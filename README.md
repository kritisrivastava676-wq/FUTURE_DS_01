# 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes business sales data for a regional Heavy Electricals and Inverter business to transform raw transaction records into actionable business intelligence. The goal is to identify top revenue-generating products, track performance trends over time, and discover which product categories drive the most profitability to guide future business growth.

## 🎯 Answering the Core Business Questions

* **Which products generate the most revenue?** While everyday items (like 9W LED Bulbs) have the highest transaction volume, the **1200Ah Industrial Battery Bank** and **900Ah Home Backup Systems** generate the vast majority of total financial revenue.
* **How do sales change over time?** Sales show massive revenue spikes specifically on days when heavy industrial equipment is sold, highlighting a project-based revenue cycle rather than a steady retail cycle.
* **Which categories are most profitable?** The **Heavy Power** category is the absolute core economic engine of the business, far outperforming the combined revenue of Lighting, Wiring, and Switches.
* **Where should the business focus to grow faster?** Reallocate marketing to B2B direct trade partnerships with regional construction contractors, automatically bundle high-capacity inverters with premium armored cables, and maintain high heavy-power stock leading into summer.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* **Python (Pandas):** Automated data cleaning, date parsing, and KPI calculation.
* **Plotly:** Interactive, presentation-ready dashboard visualizations.
* **Google Colab:** Environment for exploratory data analysis and executive reporting.
